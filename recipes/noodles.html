Noodles

Description

These stir fried noodles with ramen and veggies are a quick, easy,
and delicious recipe that all will enjoy.
Try adding cooked, cubed pork, chicken,
or any of your favorite vegetables to this versatile recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 (3 ounce) packages soy sauce-flavored ramen noodles with seasoning packets
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil, divided
  • 3 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 teaspoons vegetable oil, divided
  • 2 teaspoons vegetable oil, divided
  • 1 small carrot, peeled and grated
  • ½ cup green peas
  • ¼ cup red bell pepper, minced
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce, or to taste

Step 1

  1. Gather all ingredients.

Step 2

  1. Bring a medium pot of water to a boil.
  2. Cook ramen noodles, reserving seasoning packets, in boiling water until softened, about 3 minutes.
  3. Drain noodles and set aside.

Step 3

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon vegetable oil in a small skillet.
  2. Cook and stir beaten eggs in hot oil until scrambled and firm.
  3. Set aside.

Step 4

  1. Heat 1 teaspoon vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium heat.
  2. Add green onions into hot oil and stir fry until softened, 2 to 3 minutes.
  3. Remove green onions to a plate; set aside.

Step 5

  1. Heat remaining 1 teaspoon vegetable oil in the same wok or skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add carrots, peas, and bell pepper separately; stir frying until softened, removing each to the plate with green onions when done.

Step 6

  1. Combine 2 tablespoons sesame oil with remaining 1 tablespoon vegetable oil in the same skillet or a wok over medium heat.
  2. Stir fry cooked noodles in hot sesame-vegetable oil, tossing frequently, until golden, 3 to 5 minutes.
  3. Season with desired amounts of sesame oil, soy sauce, and reserved ramen seasoning packets; toss to coat.

Step 6

  1. Stir in cooked vegetables, tossing frequently, until heated through, about 5 more minutes.

Step 7

  1. Serve and Enjoy!
